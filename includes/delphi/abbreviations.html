Abbreviations List for Study

  • EMR - Endoscopic Mucosal Resection
  • ER - Endoscopic Resection
  • ESD - Endoscopic Submucosal Dissection
  • Competent ER pracitioner - a person deemed competent to perform endoscopic resection procedures independently and deal with complications
  • Supervising practitioner - the trainer and endoscopist/consultant responsible for the patient
  • Lesion - a polyp, laterally spreading tumour or laterally spreading lesion under consideration for endoscopic resection
  • LSL - laterally spreading lesion, considered a polyp >=20mm in maximum dimension that is of the Paris 0-Ix type
  • SMIC - submucosal invasive cancer
  • Non-lifting sign - when attempting submucosal injection beneath a target lesion no lifting of the mucosa is seen despite adequate positioning of the injection needle and repeated attempts
  • Jet sign - rapid and forceful ejection of injectate tangential to the lesion on attempted submucosal injection
  • Mucin cap - please provide a definition
